# Formatting
* At the end of the Lenten opening acclamation, the template field {cross} appears, rather than the character ✠.
* Rather than have 24 pt of spacing before the paragraph in the “Heading” style, let’s just prepend two blank spacer lines before “Heading” styles whenever they are used.
* In the Prayers of the People, Form IV
** After each petition is Silence and then the exchange “Lord, in your mercy, Hear our prayer.” Only the “Hear our prayer” should be in bold; the first part of the line is said by the reader, the second part by the congregation.
** There is an extra space after the final “Hear our prayer” before the “The Celebrant concludes with a suitable Collect” rubric.
* Let’s remove “Old 100th” from the “Doxology” header; it doesn’t mean anything to people and the formatting is confusing.
* The insertion of the Proper Preface is tricky. The right text is there, but it’s customary to join the text of the peace with the preceding line. This takes a little modification of the punctuation and capitalization. The correct output should be “…creator of heaven and earth, through Jesus Christ our Lord…” all in the same paragraph.
* The cross inserted in the sanctus should be after the word “he” rather than after the word “Blessed”
